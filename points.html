How good of a scorer is Jokic?

The scatterplot maps "minutes played per game" (MPG) on the x-axis vs "points per game" (PPG) on the y-axis. Jokic is denoted by the red point. You can mouseover a point to see more details about the player.

What can we see from this chart?

Jokic is one of the league's top scorers. He plays a lot of minutes which helps him score more per game, but being able to maintain such high PPG while consistently playing high minutes for most of the season is no easy feat.
