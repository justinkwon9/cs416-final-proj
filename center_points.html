How does Jokic fare against other centers?

The scatterplot maps "minutes played per game" (MPG) on the x-axis vs "points per game" (PPG) on the y-axis but is filtered by players that are centers. Jokic is denoted by the red point. You can mouseover a point to see more details about the player.

What can we see from this chart?

Jokic is the 3rd best scoring center in the league. Joel Embiid's stats are phenomenal, but Jokic is a more efficient scorer averaging 6 fewer field goal attempts per game with an effective field goal % of 66.0% vs Embiid's 57.3%. Jokic has also played over 10 more games than Anthony Davis who is ranked 2nd. We've established that Jokic is not only one of the best scoring centers, but one of the best scorers in the league.
